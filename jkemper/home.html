Welcome!

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Economics at Texas Tech University. I also coordinate our dedicated team of graduate instructors and teaching assistants, and I lead a weekly workshop aimed at enhancing their teaching techniques and effectiveness. I am also an adjunct instructor at the Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center. My research interests include healthcare economics, with a focus on quantifying quality of life and the value of extended life years.

Before joining Texas Tech, I was an Assistant Professor of Economics at South Plains College for eleven years. Prior to academia, I worked in finance as an investment banker during the Great Recession. I'm passionate about teaching economics and am excited to be a part of such an innovative and research-focused department. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions!
